Gabatarwa game da tsari mai kewaye ko maimaita kansa (looping structure).

Abubuwan da muka koya abaya sun kara mana haske game da yadda ake Amfani da wasu tsarikan wajen sanya ka'idoji dama sharruda masu dan Sauki acikin manhaja.Saboda haka sanin tsarikannan na babin baya kadai, Zai iya bamu damar rubuta irin tsarin manhajar da mukeso cikin sauki da kwarewa.Idan muka sake yin waiwaye game da misalin mu nabaya da ya Wuce na Haidar da yar uwarsa Maimuna,bayan kirkirar wancen manhajar Da Haidar yayi mata saitace yanzu kuwa manhajar yana yin aiki da kyau Kuma har ma yakarasa kasan a'artatatabunka sa sosai amma tana son a Sabunta manhajar domina sanya dabarar da zair inka kirga jimilar kudin da Shagon nata yake samarwa a kowacce sati dama wasu lissafe-lissafen na Daban. Awannan sabon tsarin manhajar,sai kuwa Haidar yayi amfani da Ilminsa nasanin tsari mai kewaye kot sari mai maimaita kansa(repetation structure) a wajen aiwatar da wannan aiki. Yadda manhajar zai yi aiki kuwa shine da farko zai tambayi abokin Cinikayya misali bari muce Maimuna daga ranar Litinin harzuwa Lahadi sannan shikuma Manhajarsa na iiyalissafa yawan kudin da aka samu asati sannan ya bayyana sakamakon.Alhalikuwa ana so Haidar yayi amfani da layi daya kuwa ya rinka Kewayowa yana bada damar saka yawan kudin kowacce rana daga mai Amfani dashi har sau bakwai.To abin tambayar shine tayaya za’ayi Haidar yayi wannan da ba kira'tsari mai kewaye'ko'tsari mai maimaita kansa' (repetition structure).

Tsari maikewaye komai maimaita kansa (repetition structure): Tsarine

da yake sanya maimaituwar karanta layin umarni guda har sau wani adadin da makirkiri (programmer)yasanya masa yayin gina manhajar.

Lalle wannan tsarin amai kewaye ko mai maimaita kansa yana taimakawa matuka a wajen sanya manhaja ta rin kamaimaita aikin ta har sau wani Adadi mai yawa domin aiwatar da wani umarni. Sannan yana taimakawa a Wajen takaice layikan umarni masu yawazuwalayikayankadanyayingina manhaja,sannankodawanigyaraza'ayiacikinlayikanumarni(codes)na manhajar,tokuwagyararzaikasanceneawajegudakawaikumabatare daanshawatawahalasosaiba.Wannankewayawarkomaimaituwada manhajarkeyitadalilinamfanidalayinumarnigudashiakekiradasuna 'Looping'afanninharsunankirikira(programminglanguages),kumaabune sananneawannanfanni.Ire-irentsarimaikewayekomaimaimaitakansa yakasukashibiyu;sune:tsarimaikewayemaiamfanidasharadinka'ida (conditional),dakumatsarimaikewayemaiamfanidasharadin'counter'. Dukkansuwadannantsarikanzamuyidubiacikinsuawannanbabinin Allahyayarda.

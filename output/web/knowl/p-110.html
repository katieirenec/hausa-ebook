2 da kuma gurbin da zai rinka yin lissafi irinsu; hadawa, debewa, ninkawa, dama gurbin da zai rinka aiwatar da wasu nau’ikan lissafe-lissafen na daban ko dai gurbin da ake son ya aiwatar da guda daga wadancen lissafin kawai wanda wancen fasasshen na’urar lissafin nashi ke iya aiwatarwa. Saboda haka, sai kawai ka kudiri niyyar kirkirar wannan manhajar lissafin. Na san cewar a take zaka tuna da cewar wannan tsarin manahajar yana bukatar maimata wasu kalmomi na umarni (codes) lokaci bayan lokaci a wajen rubuta wannan manhajar. Misali. Idan aka so a karbi lambobi biyu sannan a hadasu ko kuma aka so ninka su a wani gefen, ko dai ake son a gabatar da wani nau’in lissafin na daban da su wadannan lambobin ba tare da an cenzasu ba. Saboda haka; domin yin wannan aikin, a shawarce kayi amfani da tsarin “Dabarar Function”. Domin wannan tsarin shine kawai zai baka damar yin hakan cikin sauki da kuma kwarewa, domin kuwa kowacce dabarar lissafin da ka saka shine yake rike da wannan nashi kalar dabarar lissafin, kawai idan ana so ya aiwatar da nashi lissafin to kawai sai a kirashi domin yin hakan. A takaice dai, shi dababar function yana bada dama ne ayi amfani da bayani guda sannan a fadi yadda ake son ayi ta sarrrafa wannan bayani ta hanyoyi daban-daban ba tare da an cenza wadancen bayanan da wasu ba. Misali. Idan aka shigo da lambobi biyu sai kuma ake son a hada wadannan lambobin sannan a wani bangaren kuma a ninka wadannan lambobin sannan a wani bangaren kuma a debe wadannan lambobi kuma ba tare da an maimaita rubuta kowacce umarni ba ma’ana ana son a rubuta dukkansu umarnin a waje guda, to babu wani tsari mai sauki da zai baka damar yin hakan sai dabarar function. Misali. Kaine kake don idan mutum ya shigo da lamba biyu, kana son a hadasu (Add) ko a rabasu (Subtract) ko dai a ninkasu (Multiply) kuma baka son rubuta umarninsu a waje daban-daban sai a waje guda to kawai sai kayi amfani da dabarar function a wajen yin wannan.

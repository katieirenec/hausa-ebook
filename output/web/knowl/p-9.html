Duk da cewar shi yaren ‘Assembly’ shima turanci ba 0 da 1 ba, to amma komfiwta yana fahimtarsa cikin sauri fiye da sauran yarukan yan adam domin shi an sanya wakilcin kowacce kalmace da taron 0 da 1. Ma’ana an riga da an fassara masa shi komfiwtar cewar ga wasu kalmomi idan aka yi amfani da su to ga ma’anarsa a Binary, saboda haka idan komfiwta ya karanta irin wadannan kalmomin zai fahimcesu cikin sauri fiye da sauran yarukan dan adam domin dama akwai ma’anarsu a kwakwalwarsa.

Game da yadda na’urar komfiwta ke daukar wadannan jerin umarni a matsayin yaren dan adam (High level language) sannan ya sarrafasu ko ya juyasu zuwa yaren na’urar komfiwta (low level language) to kuwa sai dai in takaice bayanina domin wannan littafin ba zai iya daukar wannan bayanan dukkansu ba sai dai a takaita. Abinda ake nufi da ‘high level language’ ko muce yaren da dan adam ke iya fahimta shine, duk wani yare da dan adam ke amfani da shi a matsayin hanyar zayyana ko isar da sako.

Kalmar'Dountil' Amfaninwannankalmar'Dountil'kusandayanedakalmar'Dowhile' dominkuwakalmar'Dountil'zaibadadamaraikinmanhajaryacigabada kewayowaharsaisharadinka'idaryazamaE(True)sannanzaidakata. Ammashikuwakalmar'Do while'manhajarzaidingakewayowane matukarsharadinka'idarkatazamantoE(True)tsarinsakusangudaneda nakalmar'Dowhile'.Atsarinkalmar'Dountil'da'Dowhile'asalinlayikan umarnindaakabawamanhajarnezaifaraaikatuwasannanyaauna sharadinka'idadominyatantanceshinzaisakekewayowakokuwazai dakataanan,sabodahakaakallazaiaikatusaudayakafinfaraauna sharadinka'idar. Shikalmar'Dowhile'kewayowarsayadangantanedasaiidanansamu amsarsharadinka'idarmutazamaE(True)ammashikuwa'Dountil'sai sharadinka'idartazamtoA'ane(False)dominsakesabonkewayowa. Fa'ida: Ananidankadubazakagacewarlayikanumarnindaakabawamanhajar neyafaraaiwatarwawatoyarubuta"Sakakudindaakasamuakowacce rana"sannandagabayasaiakabashisharadindaakesoyacikanaka'ida kafinyasakekewayowa,watoshinesaiidanmafarin_ka'idayayikasako yayidai-daida7sannanzaisakezagayowaidanbahakabakuwaya dakata.Watodaiakalmar'Dountil'saiamsarsharadinka'idarmutazamto A'ane(False)sannanzaikewayoidankuwaakasamuE(True)amatsayin amsarsharadinka'idartokuwakewayowarzaidakata. (Sabodahakasaikayinazarisosaiawajenbambancetsakaninsudomin wanilokacisunarikitarwa).

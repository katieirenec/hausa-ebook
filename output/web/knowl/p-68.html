Paragraph

To bari mu waiwayi misalinmu na cen baya a game din da Ali yana dawowa daga makaranta. A misalin an ce ana so Ali ya kauce matukar ya ga dutse ko ruwa a hanyarsa. To yaya za a yi a sanya wannan ka'idar a wajen kirkirar manhaja cewa matukar Ali ya ga dutse ko ruwa? To kuwa hakan zai iya yiwuwane ta hanyar amfani da wasu dabaru da ake kira 'tsarin lissafi na dabaru'.

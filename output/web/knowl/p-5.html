Menene kirkira?, sannan ta yaya zan iya fara yin kirkira? Saboda haka, ina dai tsammanin ka kasance a wannan wajen ne domin wannan manufa. Koda yake, na san zaka iya tuna wasu abubuwa a game da shaâ€™anin kirkirar manhaja matukar ka dan taba karanta wani abu akan shaâ€™anin kirkira koda kadanne. Koma dai yayane, wannnan littafin zai kasance maka wani haske ne da zai kasance mafarin sanin wannan harkan na kirkira. Saboda haka; ina tsammanin a karshen wannan littafin zaka fahimci abubuwa akan wannan fanni na kirkiran manhaja, sannnan ina tsammanin harma ka samu damar iya kirkirar naka manhajar.

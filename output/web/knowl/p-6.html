A wani bangaren littafin kuma, za a haskaka sauran dalibaine hanaya a game da wannan hanyar na kirkirar manhaja a wannan duniya tamu ta kirkire-kirkire masu ban al’ajabi. To amma yanzu bari in fara da amsa wancen tambaya da aka yi dazu, wato menene kirkira? Kirkira wani hanyace na samar da wasu sinkin umarni da na’urar komfiwta zai aiwatar da. Taron umarni da suke aiwatar da wani aiki na musamman su ake kira da ‘program’. Mutumin da yake rubuta wannan ‘program’ (aiki) shi ake kira da ‘programmer’ wato makirkiri. Sannan ana iya kiran kalmar ‘programmer’ wato makirkiri da makirkirin manhaja wato ‘software developer’. To amma dai duk wannan ba shine abin tambaya a wajen wanda yake sabo a wannan babin bane ba. Tambayarsa ba zai wuce ta yaya na’urar komfiwta ke iya daukar wadannan jerin umarnin da makirkirn ya rubuta sannan ya sarrafasu harma ya aiwatar da ayyuka da dama a cikin wannan jerin umarnin ba. Wannan dama sauran amsar wasu tambayoyin da ba a yisu anan ba zasu zo a nan gaba kadan a wannan littafin in Allah ya yarda.

Misalin irin wadannan yarukan sune yarukan mu na yau da kullum kamarsu Hausa, Larabci, Turanci, Siwahili, Urdu, Chainanci, Indiyanci da dai sauransu. Amma shi kuwa ‘Low level language’ wato yaren na’urar komfiwta shine irin yaren da na’urar komfiwta ke iye fahimta domin aiwatar da wani umarni. Wannan yare nasa a wani sunan shi ake kira da ‘Binary’ kuma abu biyune kacal a wannan yare nasa sune kuwa 0 da 1. Na’urar komfiwta yana hada wadannan jeren 0 da 1 domin wakiltar wani harafi ko lamba a namu yaren yan adam. Misali, a yaren “binary”, 1 yana nufin E (Yes), ko Gaskiya (True), ko kunnawa (On) sannan 0 kuma shine A’a (No), ko karya (False), ko kashewa (Off) a wajensa. Dan sama kadan da “binary” a kwai “assembly.” Wannan yaren na “assembly” yana kosa da “binary” a bangaren gane wa na na’urar komfiwta.

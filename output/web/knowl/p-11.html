Gaba daya irin wadannan na’urorin suna da sassa na zahiri da suka hadu suka samar da su. Sassa na zahiri (Hardware) sune abubuwan da suka hadu suka samar da na’urar komfiwta. Sune irin sassan da ake iya gani, a taba harma a ji shi. Cikakken na’urar komfiwta yana da sassa irinsu Sashen Masarrafar Bayanai (SMB) wato ‘Central Processing Unit (CPU)’, Ma’aji (Memory), kananan na’urori masu shigar da ko fitar da bayanai (Input and Output devices), da kuma Ma’aji da ake sakowa daga waje (Secondary storage).

Kalmar'Dowhile' Wannanshimadaiaikinsakamarnakalmar'while'dinyaketosaidaishi indayadanbambantadawancenkalmarshine;atsarinkalmar'Dowhile' saimanhajaryafaraaiwatardalayikanumarnindaakasanyamasafarko kafinyajeyakarantasharadinka'idardaakasakamishisabaninkalmar 'While'wandadolenesaiyafarakarantasharadinka'idarkafinyaaiwatar dalayikanumarnindaakasanyamasa.Kalmar'while'sharadinka'idashine farisannanlayikanumarniammakalmar'Dowhile'kuwaumarnifarko sannansharadinka'ida. Amfanidawadannankalmominbiyunataimakawamatukaawajenkirkirar manhajojiiri-irisannancikinsaukidakwarewadominsukansamarda tsarinfitadagamanhajayayindamutumyasanyawasunau'ikanrubutuna musamman. Nau'ikanrubutunamusamman(sentinelvalues):Wasualamominena rubutunamusammandaidanakasanyasualokacindaakeaikida manhajakanjanyodakatardamanhajataredafitadagatsarindazaibaka damarsakekewayowa. Dukkansukalmominbiyu('Dowhile'da'Dountil')anaiyaamfanidasua wajenyinaikiirigudamatukarsoakeasamutsarimaikewayesamada gudaamanhaja.

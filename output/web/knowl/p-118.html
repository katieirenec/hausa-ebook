7 A duk lokacin da dabarar function ya kewayo a aikinsa, to kuwa wancen wajen da yake rike da bayanin da aka miko masa daga gurbi na uku shine kuma zai sake dawowa ya shiga cikin tsarin dabarar da aka tsara. Misali. idan tun farko an shigo da lambar 2 da 4 ne a wajen lissafasu kuma watakila a dabarar da aka tsara an sanya cewa zai hada wadannan lambobi sannan zai ninka wadannan lambobi ko dai wani abu kamar haka, to kuwa a duk sanda gurbin mika ‘parameters’ ya tashi mikowa to kuwa wadannan lambobin zai yi ta mikowa ana maimaita wadannan lissafin da su a wajaje daban-daban. Saboda haka; ya kamata kasan cewar za’a iya bada wakilcin wasu lambobin ga wasu harrufan domin a duk sanda aka so amfani da wadannan lambobin, to kuwa sai a kira wadannan harruffan su kuwa sai su miko lambobin da aka wakilta musu ga gurbin zayyana dabaru da aka tsara, shi kuma sai yayi amfani da wadannan lambobin domin aiwatar da dabarun da aka sanya masa yayi. To sai dai fa ya kamata ka san cewar sharadin da zai bada damar mika wadannan harrufan da suke wakiltar wasu lambobin to kuwa dolene sai wadannan harrufan nau’in bayaninsu (data type) dolene sai ya dace da nau’in tsarin dabarar da aka tsara a gurbin tsara dabaru. Yanzu kam, na san ka dan fahimci yadda ake tsara dabarun function a yayin kirkirar manhaja, sannan a gefe guda kuwa yana da matukar muhimmanci ka san cewar akwai wani hanyar tsara dabarar function din kuma wanda baya bukatar ka zayyana irin dabarun da zai maka kawai dai an riga da an sanya masa yadda zai aiwatar da duk wata dabara da taimakon sanya sunan nau’in dabarar a farkon layikan umarni na manhajar. Wadannan abubuwan kuwa sune ake kira da “Library functions”.

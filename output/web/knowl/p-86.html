Paragraph

Lalle wannan tsarin amai kewaye ko mai maimaita kansa yana taimakawa matuka a wajen sanya manhaja ta rin kamaimaita aikin ta har sau wani Adadi mai yawa domin aiwatar da wani umarni. Sannan yana taimakawa a Wajen takaice layikan umarni masu yawazuwalayikayankadanyayingina manhaja,sannankodawanigyaraza'ayiacikinlayikanumarni(codes)na manhajar,tokuwagyararzaikasanceneawajegudakawaikumabatare daanshawatawahalasosaiba.Wannankewayawarkomaimaituwada manhajarkeyitadalilinamfanidalayinumarnigudashiakekiradasuna 'Looping'afanninharsunankirikira(programminglanguages),kumaabune sananneawannanfanni.Ire-irentsarimaikewayekomaimaimaitakansa yakasukashibiyu;sune:tsarimaikewayemaiamfanidasharadinka'ida (conditional),dakumatsarimaikewayemaiamfanidasharadin'counter'. Dukkansuwadannantsarikanzamuyidubiacikinsuawannanbabinin Allahyayarda.

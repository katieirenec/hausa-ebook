3 Shi dabarar function yana da bangaren da ake kira da bangaren sama ko mu kirasu da suna ‘yan sama’ da kuma bangaren ‘yan tsakiya’. Shima bangaren yan saman ya kasu har kashi uku. Sune; gurbin zayyana nau’in yan aike (return type), da gurbin da zai kunshi sunan dabarar function din (function name), da kuma gurbin da zasu zauna jiran karbar sako daga sashen bangaren ‘yan aike’ da suke bangaren ‘yan tsakiya’ (Parameters). Sannan shima bangaren yan tsakiya na dababar function shima ya kasu har kashi biyu; sune: bangaren inda aikin dabarar function din yake da kuma banagaren da ake kira da yan aike. Gurbin zayyana nau’in yan aike (Return type): wannan bangare ne da yake nuna irin nau’in dan aiken da za’a aiko daga sashen yan tsakiya na dabarar function. Wato zai nuna mana cewar duk dan aiken da za a aiko daga sashen yan tsakiya na dabarar function zai zama lambace (Integers) ko harrufane (characters) ko dai lambobi masu goyone (floating point numbers) da sauransu. Banagren sunan dabarar function ne zai amsa wannan alhakin. Gurbin zayyana sunan dabarar function (function name): Abu na gaba da yake bin gurbin da yake nuna nau’in dan aiken function shine wannan gurbin na zayyana sunan dababar function. Wannan gurbine da ake sanya sunan da shine za a kira dan aiken da yake rike da sakon da ake son ya isarwa wannan guirbin shi. Gurbin da zasu zauna jiran zuwan sako daga sashen bangaren ‘yan aike’ da suke bangaren ‘yan tsakiya’ (Parameters): Bayan gurbin da ya gabata to kuwa sai wannan gurbin. Wannan gurbine da zai kunshi wasu jerin rubutu da suke zaman jiran zuwan sako daga sashen yan tsakiya na function. Kana da zabi domin sanya wannan bangare ma’ana ba dole bane sai an sanya wannan bangaren idan ka so. Sannan zaka iya sanya wadannan ‘parameters’ din da dan dama idan kana so. Su wadannan ‘parameters’ din wasu abubuwane da ake mikasu ga sashen inda aka tsara dabarar function wato inda aka zayyana yadda za’a gabatar da dabarar da ka shirya domin komfiwta ya aiwatar maka. Saboda haka; duk abinda aka saka a wannan bangaren to kuwa a cen sashen dabarar da ka shirya da shine zai yi amfani da su a wajen shirya dabarar. To ya kamata ka san cewar ana mika wadannan ‘parameters’ dinne ta hanyoyi biyu; ta hanyar amfani da lamba kai tsaye (pass by value) ko kuma ta hanyar amfani da wani harafi da zai wakilce shi (pass by reference).

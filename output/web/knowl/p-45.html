To yanzu anan,sai muce 'Ali yana dawowa daga makaranta' shine misalin tsari da yake bi daki-daki kamar yadda muka koya a baya a babi na biyu, domin kuwa Ali yana tafiyarsa ne taku bayan taku ba tareda ya tsallake wani waje ba.To amma yayinda yaga dutse a hanyarsa kuwa sai hakan zai sanya shi ya sake hanya domin kaucewa dutsen to wannan kuma shine misalin tsari mai zabe. Saboda haka tsari mai zabe ana iya sanya shi a ko'ina domin zabar matakinda ya fi dacewa da ka'idar da aka saka.

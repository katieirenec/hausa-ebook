Kalmar'While' Wannan kalmaramfaninsashineyarinkasanyaaikin manhajayana zagayowakomaimaituwabataredaansanyamasaadadindaakesoya tsayaba,kawaidaidolenefarkonsharadinka'idarkayakasanceEne(True) kafinmanhajaryasakekewayowaammayayindasharadinyajuyazuwa A'a(False),tokuwamanhajarzaidakatadagakewayendayakeyi. Bakamarakalmar'for'damukayibayaniabayabaindashiwannankalma na'while'yakedabangarebiyukawai,sune;bangarenfarkoshinegurbin sharadinka'ida.Wannanbangare shinemanhajarkeaunawafarkodomin sanincewakosharadindaakesoyacikashi(True)kokuwaa'a(False) kafinyashigafaraaikinkewayowa.Sannanbangarenabiyukumashine asalingundarinumarnindaakasanyamasadayaaiwatarkumashinezai yitamaimaitashisauwasuadadimasuyawasabaninkalmar'for'daake sanyamasasharadinfarawadakumasharadindakatarwa. GakadandagamisalinyaddamanhajarHaidartakeidanzamuyiamfanida kalmar'while'.

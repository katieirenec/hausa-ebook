Sashen Masarrafar Bayanai (SMB) wato ‘Central Processing Unit (CPU)’ shine kwakwalwar na’urar komfiwta, shine yake kula da duk wasu aikace- aikacen da ke faruwa a cikin na’urar komfiwtar wannan kuwa ya shafi aiwatar da duk wani aiki ko aikin manhaja. Zai karbi bayani daga waje, sannan ya aiwatar da lissafi sannan ya mika sakamakon lissafin. Hoton Sashen masarrafar bayanai (CPU).

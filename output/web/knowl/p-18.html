6 adam (high level language) gaba dayansa sannan ya fassara shi zuwa yaren da yafi kusa da yaren komfiwta (Machine language). Saboda haka wannan fassararren rubutun za a iya amfani da shi a koda yaushe idan aka bukaci hakan. Amma shi kuwa ‘Interpreter’ yana fassara layi daya-dayane a cikin rubutun, wato layi bayan layi sabanin shi kuma ‘Compiler’ da yake fassara gaba daya rubutun a lokaci guda. Wannan tsarin haka zai yi ta tafiya har zuwa karshen aikin manhajar. A karshe dai, yanzu na tabbatar dai kana da kyakkyawar fahimta a game da sassan na’urar komfiwta dama yadda suke aiwatar da ayyukokinsu har dama yadda komfiwta ke aiwatar da tsare-tsarensa. Domin tuna maka mai karatu, wannnan littafin zai maida hankaline akan yadda tsare-tsaren manhaja yake kasancewa ko kuma muce yadda ake aiwatar da dabaru iri-iri a wajen samar da manhaja amma ba zamu maida hankalinmu akan wani harshen kirkira guda daya kawai ba, sai dai zamu maida hankaline akan yadda zaka fahimci yadda ake tsara manhaja a tsarin rubutun ‘pseudocode’. Tsarin rubutu na ‘pseudocode’ wani tsarin rubutu ne da yake bada damar zayyana yadda tsarin rubutun kirkira yake ba tare da kula da ka’idoji ko dokokin rubutu a yaren kirkira ba. Saboda haka; a wajen tsara rubutun ‘pseudocode’, baka bukatar sanya wasu alamomi da ake amfani da su a wajen kirkira domin kawai shi tsarin rubutun ‘pseudocode’ rubutu ne kawai da zaka iya yi da kowane irin yare da kake iya fahimta. Kamar dai kaine aka ce ka rubuto hanyoyin da ake bi a wajen dafa shinkafa, to kaga kuwa anan, zaka fara ne da cewa na (1) A hura wuta, (2) A daura tukunya da ruwa akan wutan, (3) Sannan a wanke shinkafa a zuba aciki, (4) Idan ya dahu a kwashe. To fa wannan misalin rubutun shi ake kira da tsarin rubutu na ‘pseudocode’. To kuwa hakama za a rinka zayyano hanyoyin da za’a bi domin kirkirar wata manhaja amma a rubuce. (To amma me ake nufi da dokokin kirkira wato ‘syntax’ ?) To dai duk wasu dabaru akan sanyawa tsarin rubutu na ‘pseudocode’ ne.

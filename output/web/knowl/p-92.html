➡Gurbinkaddarakarshensharadinka'ida. Awannanbangarenaukukuwa;anakaddarayawankewayenedaakeson manhajarzaiyisannanyadakata.Misalikamaramisalinmudayagabata, idankadubagurbinkaddarakarshensharadinka'idar'Ranaku'zakagaan kaddarakarshensharadinka'idarneda7wandahakanyananunacewar lalleneakowaccekewayowarmanhajar,todolenesaiyaaunasabon sharafinka'idarna'Ranaku'da7 wandaidanharsabonsharadinyakai8 watoyawuce7tosaiyadakatardamanhajardagasakewanikewayowa taredashigakarantajerinwasulayikanumarnidabasushafitsarimai kewayebadasukeasauransassanmanhajar. Ammaidansabonsharadinka'idarbaizarceabindaakesoba,tosaiya shigakarantalayikanumarnidasukecikintsarimaikewayensunewajen dazaibadadamarsakajimilaryawankudindaakasamuakowaccerana sannanyagudanardadukwasulissafe-lissafetaredabayyanasakamako.

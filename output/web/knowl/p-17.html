5 da zai bada damar rubuta tsare-tsaren manhajar da yare na musamman na dan adam (High level language). Yare na musamman na dan adam (High level language) yafi kusa da asalin yarukan da yan adam suke iya fahimta sannan yana taimakawa a wajen takaice miliyoyin kalmomin yaren Binary (wato 0 da 1) zuwa takaitattun layika a yare na musamman na dan adam (High level language). Wannan tsarin yana taimakawa makirkirin manhaja a wajen rubuta tsare-tsaren manhaja mai ma’ana ba tare da damuwa da yawan kalmomin Binary ba. Kuma dai wannan tsarin na amafani da yaren dan adam na musamman (High level language) a wajen rubuta tsare-tsaren manhaja yana taimakawa makirkiri a wajen fahimtar yadda tsarin manhajar zai kasance maimakon maida hankalinsa zuwa fahimtar yadda sashen masarrafar bayanai (CPU) yake aiki, domin kuwa kafin ka iya rubuta tsare-tsaren manhaja da yaren na’ura to kuwa dolene sai ka fahimci dabarun da sashen masarrafar bayanai (CPU) yake bi a aiwatar da aikace-aikacensa. Harsunan da ake rubuta tsare-tsaren manhaja da su kamarsu; ‘Assembly language’ duk da cewar ba za mu daukeshi a matsayin yaren komfiwta ba kai-tsaye, to amma yana daga irin harsunan da ya fi kusa da yaren komfiwta fiye da sauran harsunan, sannan duk da cewar ba taron 0 da 1 bane, to amma fa yana da dan wahala a wajen rubuta tsare-tsaren manhaja fiye da sauran harsunan da ake kiransu da ‘yarukan na’ura na musamman’ (high level language) sannan yana matukar bukatar fahimtar yadda sashen masarrafar bayanai (CPU) yake aiwatar da tsare-tsarensa a wajen aiwatar da ayyuka. (Misali na rubutun yaren ‘Assembly’). Saboda shi fa sashen masarrafar bayanai (CPU) yana fahimtar yarensa ne kawai wato ‘Binary’ a wajen aiwatar da umarni saboda haka idan aka rubuta tsare-tsaren manhaja da yaren ‘Assembly’ to kuwa akwai mai fassara wannan yaren zuwa yaren da komfiwta ke iya fahimta, wannan mai fassarar kuwa shi ake kira da ‘Assembler’ (hoton yadda aikin yake kasancewa). Domin saukaka al’amarin kuma duk wani tsare-tsaren manhajar da aka rubuta da yarukan kirkira irinsu; C, C++, JAVA, PYTHON, VISUAL BASIC, da dai sauransu sai an fassara su. Domin su wadannan yarukan basa daga nau’ikan yarukan da suka fi kusa da yaren komfiwta (Machine codes). Wadanda suke wannan fassarar su ake kira da ‘Compiler’ da ‘Interpreter’.

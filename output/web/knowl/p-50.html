Na san zaka iya tuna yadda ake lissafi da kwatanta matsayin wata lamba da wata lambar yayin lissafi musammanma a ajin darasin lissafi (Maths).Shima dai wannan tsarin da zamu yi magana akai kamar hakan yake sannan wani lokacin suna nan kamar tsarin na Boolean ne. A Wannan tsarin na kwatanta matsayin abu da wani abun (wannan abun zai iya kasancewa lamba, harrafi, kalma d.s) a lissafi ana amfani da wasu harrufane na musamman a wajen yin irin wannan lissafin.

Domin farawa bari muyi dubi zuwa ga ma’anar wannan tsari na dabarun functions. Dabarar function wani tsari ne na umarni da aka tsaro shi domin ya aiwatar da wani aiki na musamman. Misali, mu dauka cewar abokinka ya fada maka cewar na’urar lissafinsa (calculator) ya fashe kuma ya lalace sannan sakon da ya bayar domin a kawo masa sabo ba zai samu ba sai nan da sati na gaba, to kuma dama kai da abokin naka kuna amfani ne da na’urar lissafi da yake aiki iri guda, saboda ba zai yiwu kaje kasuwa ka sayo kowane irin na’urar lissafi ba sai irin wancen ga kuma abokin naka yace maka yana son kammala wannan aikin nashi ne a kankanin lokaci. To kuma kana dan shakkar bashi naka na’urar aro saboda kaima kana aiki da shi kuma idan kuka ce zaku yi amfani da naka dukkanku to hakan zai janyo rashin saurin aikin naku. Saboda haka, sai kayi wani dan tunani na cewar bari ka kirkira masa manhajar da zai dinga lissafi kamar na wancen na’urar lissafin nashi da ya lalace to amma kuma sai ka tuna da cewar hakan zai baka wahala duba da karancin saninka a harkar kirkira (Programming) saboda aikine da zai zama mai dan yawa da kuma yake bukatar sanya dabaru iri-iri. Misali, kamarsu gurbin da zai rinka karbar lambobi

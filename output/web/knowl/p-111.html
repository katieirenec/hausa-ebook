A yanzu kam na tabbatar cewar mun dan san wani abu a game da dabarar function. Saboda haka; yanzu bari mu yi magana akan yadda tsarin wannan dabarar na function yake da kuma yadda ake sanya su a wajen rubuta umarni. A baya munce idan ana son dabarar function yayi aikinsa to kuwa ya zama wajibi idan aka gama zayyana yadda ake son dabarar zata kasance to kuma sai an kirashi domin aiwatar da aikinda aka saka mishi, to amma yaya zamu yi hakan? Da farko dai dolene mu fara da zayyana yadda muke son dabarar tamu zata kasance ko muce zaka zayyana wani irin dabarane kake son wannan tsarin zai aiwatar maka.

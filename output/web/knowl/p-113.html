Paragraph

4 . Mika ‘parameters’ ta hanyar amfani da lamba kai tsaye maimakon abinda zai wakilceshi (Pass by value): A wannan tsarin, ana tura kofin lambar da yake wannan sashen ne zuwa cen sashen tsara dabara domin dabarar yayi amfani da wannan sakon da aka turo masa wato lambar. Saboda haka, ya kamata ka san cewar duk chanjin da za’a yi a cen sashen zayyana dabaru ba zai shafi wannan gurbin na mika ‘parameters’ ba domin lambace ba wani abune ya wakilceshi ba. Dalilin shine, ai dama kofin lambar aka tura ba wakilinsa ba kuma kowacce lamba dake wancen sashen da kuma wannan sasehen suna zaman kansu ne, saboda haka dan waninsu ya cenza ba zai shafi dayan ba. . Mika ‘parameters’ ta hanyar amfani da wakilci (Pass by reference) : Wannan tsarin kuwa ana wakiltar asalin bayanin ne da yake nan gurbin ko kuma muce ana zayyanawa dabarar adreshin inda wannan bayanin yake ne saboda haka, ana son idan dabarar ya fara aiki, matukar yana neman wani bayani akan wani abu to ya je adreshin da aka kwatanta masa na wannan bayanin saboda duk wani chenji da aka yiwa asalin bayanin to shima wakilin zai samu wannan chenjin domin ai dama wakiltarsa aka yi ba kofinsa aka tura ba. Sai abinda yake bin gurbin da ya wuce shine gurbin asalin inda za’a zayyana yadda dabarar naka zai kasance (function body): Wannan gurbi kuwa, gurbine da zai rinka aiwatar da aikin da aka sanya masa yayi. Misali, kamar su hadawa, debewa, rabawa, ninkawa da kuma samar da gurbin da za’a iya shigo da wani bayani domin sanya shi cikin lissafi. Wannan gurbin shine yake nuna irin dabarar da aka tsaro a wajen aikin manhajar. Gurbin da yake zama bayan shi kuwa shine gurbin komar da aikin dabarar zuwa gurbin da ya kirashi dazu sannan ya sake mika masa da bayanan da suke cen domin sake aiwatar da dabarar da aka zayyana masa a karo na gaba. // Misali a jadawalin flochat. // Yana nuna gurbin da ake kira da kuma gurbin da shine yake kiran. Ga misalinsa kamar haka: int hadawa(int A, int B) // Wannan gurbin da ke sama, gurbine dake zayyana irin nau’in bayanin da za’a yi amfani da shi a yayin tsara dabarar function. Idan ka duba zaka ga cewar sunan dabarar shine ‘Hadawa’ kuma nau’in bayanin ‘A’ shine int wato nau’ine dake nuna cewar nau’in lambobi ce (integer) sannan na ‘B’ ma lambace.

A cen baya mun danyi bayanai game da tsarin da ake kira 'decision structure' wato tsari zaba da yake zabar umarni bisa ga ka'idar da aka samar masa sannan zai aiwatar da umarnin ne muddin amsar ya zama E ne (TRUE). To me zai faru muddin dukkan amsoshin ka'idojin ka suka zama A'A ne (FALSE)? Anan sai muce babu wani umarni da zai aiwatar. Kamar dai a wancen misalin namu na Ali munce, idan Ali ya ga dutse ko ruwa to ya kauce masa to kuwa matukar bai ga dutse ko ruwa a hanyarsa ba zai ci gaba da tafiyarsane har zuwa gida. To matsalar Ita ce me zai faru idan ya ga wani abu ba dutse ko ruwa ba? To fa shine sai ayi amfani da tsarin IF ELSE (wato tsarin auna zabi sama da guda daya).
